About Us

Founded in 2018 by legendary producer/director/actor Michael Schreier and
previous Producing Director Savannah Adams with members of the first
graduating class of Juilliard's Drama Division, Job Smith and Jeff Burns.
Promoting theater, charades and literacy by bringing a repertory of classical
productions and exceptional new works to phones. The Black Box performs
each year in over 20 cities to audiences of 25,000 and reaches more
than 15,000 students with its arts education programs.
